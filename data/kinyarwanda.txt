Imirire myiza ku mugore utwite Indyo ikwiye ku mugore utwite Abagore batwite bagomba kurya indyo yuzuye harimo: ibikomoka ku matungo, imboga, imbuto, ibinyamavuta, ibinyamafufu, ibinyamisogwe n’ibinyampeke. Bagomba gufata n’izindi nyunganiramirire bahawe na muganga. Inshuro umugore utwite agomba kurya Bagomba kongera inshuro basanzwe barya kugirango barwanye ibura ry’intungamubiri n’imyunyungugu bikenewe mu mubiri. yuzuye harimo: ibikomoka ku matungo, imboga, imbuto, ibinyamavuta, ibinyamafufu, ibinyamisogwe n’ibinyampeke. Ibiribwa bibujijwe ku mugore utwite Abagore batwite bagomba kwirinda kurya ibiryo n’ibinyobwa bibujijwe nk’ikawa, ibisindisha, ibiyobyabwenge n’itabi, cyangwa kuba hafi y’abanywa itabi kuko byangiza ubuzima bw’umwana uri munda. Imiti ibujijwe ku mugore utwite Birabujijwe gufata umuti uwo ariwo wose utanditswe na muganga kuko ushobora kugira ingaruka ku mubyeyi n’umwana. Kandi wirinde imiti ya gakondo. Kumenya imikurire y’umwana Ni byiza kumenya ibyiciro by’imikurire y’umwana kuko bidufasha gutahura no gukumira hakiri kare ibibazo byahungabanya ubuzima bwe.
